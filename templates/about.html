About Me

I am a multidisciplinary creative and emerging software developer based in New York City. Originally from the suburbs of Texas, my path took an unexpected turn when I was scouted to model while working as a dishwasher at Chipotle. That moment launched a career that has taken me around the world. I have lived in five different countries and draw constant inspiration from global culture and travel.

Now focused on merging creativity with technology, I am passionate about using software development and UX/UI design to bring imaginative ideas to life. With a foundation in fashion and photography, I see code as another medium for expression that can be both functional and beautiful.

Tech Stack

As a research assistant and software developer, I focus on human-centered design and build intuitive applications that blend creative thinking with technical skill, using tools like Python, Figma, and Blender.

Photography

I shoot on 35mm film and Polaroid, drawn to their texture and honesty. Currently, I'm photographing my own cookbook, blending my love for food, design, and visual storytelling.

Fashion

I've been featured in Vogue Italia and Elle USA, traveling internationally for fashion week. This experience has shaped my eye for aesthetics and cross-cultural communication.
